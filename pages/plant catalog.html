Basil: A popular culinary herb known for its aromatic leaves, often used in Italian and Thai cuisine.

Mint: A refreshing herb with a strong fragrance, commonly used in teas and culinary dishes.

Rosemary: A woody herb with needle-like leaves, widely used for seasoning meats and vegetables.

Thyme: A fragrant herb used in cooking, with small green leaves and a slightly earthy flavor.
